Introduction

The Grievance System is a website specially desiged to minimize the problems of students. There are many students facing problems in their day to day life regarding studies, colleges, exams, time-tables, facilities,etc. Out of which many students get success in solving their problems while some are not able to raise their issues feeling shy or discomfort.

This platform makes student's task much more easier rather than following a long tideous procedure to resolve their problems.


Purpose of the Project


  1. Many student faces problems in colleges or institutes regarding Admission, Finance, Examination, Lecture Time Table/Learning, Paper 
     Revaluation, etc. After uprising issues, some of these problems might get solved but some takes immense time.
  2. Solving the problems by creating the platform to represent their issues or grievances to the staffs or institute via this system. 
  3. This System will pave the way for those students who feels shy or think their problems will not be solved.
  4. Grievance Management committee will update system according to resolved complaints and also students can rate this resolutions.
